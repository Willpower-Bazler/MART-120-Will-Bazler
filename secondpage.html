Story/Artwork

Story:

I’m dreaming. I’m in my basement, but of course it looks nothing like my basement in real life.
I have no sense of time, I don’t know what season it is, what month, what time of day, nothing.
The only light is emanating from two light bulbs hanging from the ceiling, which is only two or three feet above my head.
I’m focused on is the never ending hallway going off into the distance in front of me, with doors only on the ride side going on forever.
Strangely, there’s a random stack of wet wooden planks on my left. On my right is a locked pantry and behind me is a wall.
A t rex pokes its head out of the first door in the hallway.
